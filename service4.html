Established in 1980 we are one of the leading companies in the Maharashtra specialising in demolition & industrial dismantling of all types of buildings and structures, together with all related works including asbestos removal, asbestos surveys, site clearance, and concrete crushing. Established in 1980, FHS has built up an extensive portfolio of experience in all aspects of Demolition, Industrial Dismantling and connected services, including Asbestos Removal, Asbestos Surveys, Concrete Crushing, and Site Clearance. Our impressive portfolio of projects range from the straight-forward to the very complex, with contract values ranging from 10 lacs to 2000 lacs. As we are locate in the Mumbai, the majority of our work falls within the State and in and around Maharashtra. We do however provide a service throughout India. Our experience allows us up-to-date with the many legal requirements involved in demolition and dismantling works including all relevant health and safety, environmental, building control, and planning permission related legislation.
